About

Public records, plainly published.

An independent, journalist-run public-records publication for the state of Georgia.

Our mission

Georgia's Open Records Act (Ga. Code 50-18-72) makes arrest and booking records public because transparent accountability of the criminal justice system is a cornerstone of a functioning democracy. GeorgiaBooked publishes those records in a searchable, verifiable, and navigable format, so that Georgians can follow what is happening in their own counties.

Editorial standards

  • Factual only.

    Each entry reflects public records from county sheriff offices or verified aggregators. No speculation, no editorial commentary on guilt or character.

  • Presumption of innocence.

    Every arrest page carries a visible disclaimer. An arrest is an allegation, not a conviction.

  • Prompt corrections.

    Factual errors are corrected within 72 hours. Expunged or dismissed records are removed on verification.

  • No pay-to-remove.

    We do not accept payment to remove records. Takedowns are free and reviewed on the merits.

Methodology

Booking data is aggregated hourly from public Georgia sources. Each entry is summarized into neutral, factual prose using a large language model (Gemini 2.5), then programmatically classified by charge severity. Mugshot photos are sourced from original publishers.
