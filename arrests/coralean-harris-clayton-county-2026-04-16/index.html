Coralean Harris

Booked Thursday, April 16, 2026

Presumption of innocence. This is a public-record booking report. Coralean Harris has been charged but not convicted. All charges are allegations and the accused is presumed innocent until proven guilty in a court of law.

Coralean Harris was booked into Clayton County, Georgia custody on April 16, 2026, and was charged with violation of probation. Harris is alleged to have violated the terms of a previously imposed probation sentence.

Violation of probation is classified as a misdemeanor offense in Georgia. When an individual is found to have violated the conditions of probation, the supervising court may impose additional penalties, which can include fines, extended probation, or incarceration depending on the nature and severity of the violation and the defendant's criminal history. The specific circumstances of the alleged violation and the original offense would typically be reviewed during a probation violation hearing.
