Mary Craven

Booked Friday, April 24, 2026

Presumption of innocence. This is a public-record booking report. Mary Craven has been charged but not convicted. All charges are allegations and the accused is presumed innocent until proven guilty in a court of law.

Mary Craven was booked into the Bulloch County jail on April 24, 2026, and was charged with misdemeanor DUI with child endangerment. According to booking records, she is alleged to have operated a motor vehicle while under the influence in a manner that endangered a child passenger.

Misdemeanor DUI charges in Georgia carry potential penalties including fines, suspension of driving privileges, mandatory alcohol education programs, and possible jail time. When child endangerment is cited as an aggravating factor in a DUI case, it typically reflects that a minor was present in the vehicle during the alleged offense. Georgia law treats DUI incidents involving children as serious misdemeanors due to the heightened risk to vulnerable passengers.
