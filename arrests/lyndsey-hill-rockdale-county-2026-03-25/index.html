Hill is alleged to have committed these offenses in connection with a traffic incident in Rockdale County.

These charges represent a combination of traffic violations and drug-related offenses classified as serious misdemeanors under Georgia law. Possession of marijuana less than one ounce is a misdemeanor in Georgia.
